Regexcellence

Regexcellence is a community-driven application for learning and practicing regular expressions through tutorials and user-submitted challenges.
Our philosophy is simple: Learn to help others learn. Regex can be daunting and confusing but with our simple 12 steps tutorial, users can directly interact with our lessons and test their regex patterns every step of the way. Our pattern matching algorithm parses and dynamically checks user inputs as they type! Once users have completed the tutorial they are open to submit their own challenges, providing additional practice problems to the rest of the RegExers!
